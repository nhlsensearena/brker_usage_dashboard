07Key findings
L1 cold start — 38% quit without passing
25 users (23%) gave up after a single attempt. 42 total never cleared L1. The drop is immediate — likely reflects unfamiliarity with VR dribbling mechanics rather than lack of motivation.
L4 is the hardest wall in the game
Pass rate drops to 53% with an average of 4.2 tries to clear — nearly 3× more than L1–L3. One user attempted L4 14 times without success. Clear difficulty spike that cuts the active user base in half.
L2 star rating not tracked
All 33 successful L2 passes recorded 0 stars. Every other level returns 1–3 stars on completion. Appears to be a data tracking issue rather than gameplay behaviour.
L9 difficulty spike — 33% pass rate
Only 1 of 3 users cleared L9 (33%), on a 2.0 avg tries. Small sample, but mirrors the L4 pattern. Upper levels may follow an every-other-level difficulty structure worth validating.
Within-session flow is strong
Median time between unlocking a level and attempting the next is ~1 minute across L1–L4. Players who pass don't hesitate — internal drive between levels is working well.
Multi-session users go 2.5× further
Users who returned reached an average of level 3.95 vs 1.60 for single-session players. The 17% who came back are meaningfully more engaged — and most returned within the same day (median 11.3h).
